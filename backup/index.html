About

  • Dr Sian Hartshorne

    MBBCh (Wits), FC Derm (SA), MMed Derm

    Dr Hartshorne is our resident Dermatologist. She will tend to all your skin related needs such as skin cancer treatment and prevention, eczema and acne treatment and mole scans.

    Dr Hartshorne is also the current president of the SASDS, a board member of the DSSA and AWDS, is serving on the South African Dermatology Congress Organising Committee for 2020/2021 and serves as an honorary dermatology consultant for the Eden district of the Western Cape

  • Dr Theo van den Handel

    MBBCh (Wits), Dip HIV Man (SA)

    Dr van den Handel is our General Practitioner. He will see to your every-day medical needs like annual check-ups and medicals. He is also able to assist with dermatological needs such as mole scans.

  • Our Products

    We supply a number of products from the Bioderma, Cetaphil, Heliocare, Epizone, Ison, La Roche Posay, Neostrata, Proactive Collagen, Opti Phi, Optoderm, Reitzers and Skinceuticals ranges.
